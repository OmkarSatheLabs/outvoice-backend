## Project Scope
OutVoice is an invoicing SaaS platform that automates invoice creation, client notifications, and collections.
*   **User & Workspace Management**: Multiple workspaces, role-based workspace members, invitation systems, default workspaces.
*   **Customer Directory**: Store billing, contact, and tax details for clients.
*   **Invoice Lifecycle**: Generate PDFs, record payments, and track statuses.
*   **Automated Dunning**: Automatically send follow-up notifications when payment deadlines are missed.
*   **Legal Compliance & Default Resource Pack**: Equip users with all necessary audit logs and resources to file legal complaints with law enforcement authorities in the event of default.
